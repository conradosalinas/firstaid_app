Seizure

Do not restrain them but put a blanket or clothing next to their head, but not under it, to protect them from injury. Placing under their head may compromise the airway. Restraining them may cause injury to you or them. Let the seizure run its normal course.

After the seizure, roll the person to their side with their head tilted back. This will make sure they keep breathing and will allow any fluids to drain from their mouth.
