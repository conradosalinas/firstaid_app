Diabetic Emergency

Diabetic

Give them something containing sugar, such as sugar tablets, orange juice, sugar-containing candy or non-diet drink.

In diabetic emergencies, blood sugar levels can become too low; this can cause people to collapse. Giving them something sugary, including candy such as Skittles, will help raise their blood sugar levels and improve bodily function. Avoid giving them a diet drink, as it won't have any sugar in it and will not help them.
